This is My Final Year Project

Virtual Tour To Sigiriya Using Metaverse Technology
For Disable People

This is my Final Year Project.

The use of Metaverse technology can provide an immersive and interactive experience in a high level regardless of the physical or sensory abilities. The motivation for this project is to address the accessibility challenges faced by disabled people. This project creates a virtual tour for disable people who can explore the Ancient Sigiriya using Metaverse technology in a safe and accessible manner using a VR headset. As well as experiencing the beauty and the history of Sigiriya including time travelling to the ancient Sigiriya with hands on experience.

    There are limited researches are available on use of Metaverse technology for disable people. When we consider about the Sri Lankan researches, it is a new chapter to the Sri Lankan technology. Therefore at the end of this project, I hope to publish a Research paper and introduce this product to the market as well.
